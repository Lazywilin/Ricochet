HOW IT WORKS
Step 01
STUDY THE CURRICULUM
Navigate to the Curriculum page and work through BBA subjects in order. Each subject has targeted MCQ sets and short question banks built around the official HSC syllabus.
Step 02
ENTER THE EXAM HALL
Test yourself under real conditions. Choose Practice, Timed, or Blitz mode. Configure questions per session and time limits. A per-question timer keeps you sharp.
Step 03
REVIEW YOUR ANALYTICS
Every session is logged. Track your score trajectory, identify weak subjects, and measure improvement over time. Data-driven preparation beats guesswork every time.
Step 04
REPEAT UNTIL PERFECT
The 142-day roadmap keeps you on pace. Follow the daily plan, stay consistent, and let the system tell you when you're ready. Confidence comes from repetition.

TACTICAL STUDY TIPS
01
◎
Active Recall
Write definitions from memory instead of re-reading. Close the book, write what you know, then check. This is 2–3x more effective than passive review.
02
⧖
Spaced Repetition
Review material at increasing intervals — 1 day, 3 days, 7 days, 14 days. Ricochet's roadmap is built around this principle automatically.
03
⚡
Timed Practice
Always practice under time pressure before the real exam. Use Blitz mode weekly to keep your MCQ speed sharp. The exam won't wait for you.
04
▤
Weak Subject First
Your brain avoids difficulty. Override it — check Analytics, find your lowest subject, and spend the first 30 minutes of every session there.
05
◈
Exam Simulation
Run at least one full 25-question Timed exam per week. Familiarity with pressure reduces exam anxiety significantly on the actual day.
06
⬡
Review Mistakes
After every exam, go through every wrong answer. Understand why you got it wrong — misread, didn't know, or silly mistake. Each has a different fix.
